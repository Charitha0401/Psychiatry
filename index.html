About Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize psychiatric diagnosis by systematically organizing and analyzing the diagnostic criteria of the DSM-5 through the lens of logical structures. We aim to bridge the gap between complex diagnostic text and practical clinical application by developing an extensive, accessible repository of DSM-5 diagnostic criteria for all psychiatric conditions. By utilizing Boolean algebra, we transform these criteria into logical circuits, providing a clear, structured, and visual approach to understanding psychiatric diagnoses. Through the application of mathematical models, we analyze these logical circuits to uncover patterns and insights that can inform future research and clinical practices. Our goal is to enhance the understanding of psychiatric conditions, aiding mental health professionals in their diagnostic processes by offering a tool that clarifies and systematically analyzes symptoms and signs. Ultimately, we aspire to contribute to mental health research with a new perspective and methodology, leading to more accurate and efficient diagnostic techniques. Our project aims to make psychiatric diagnostic criteria more understandable, analyzable, and actionable, thereby improving mental health diagnosis and treatment.
