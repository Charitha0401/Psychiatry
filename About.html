About

Charitha Nallaka

I am Charitha Nallaka, currently pursuing my Master's in Applied Statistics and Data Science at the University of Texas at Arlington (UTA). My passion for understanding mental health through data analysis led me to the "Logic of Psychiatry" project under the guidance of Professor Ogan. This project aims to develop a comprehensive database of logical structures for DSM-5 conditions, using mathematical techniques to identify patterns and create visualizations that enhance our understanding of these disorders. The project has been both challenging and rewarding, involving the extraction and organization of complex diagnostic criteria, the design of accurate logic circuits, and the integration of user-friendly technology. These experiences have honed my skills in data preprocessing, logic circuit design, web development, and effective communication. This work aligns with my aspiration to contribute to mental health research and clinical practice through innovative data-driven solutions. I am grateful to Professor Ogan, my peers, and mentors for their invaluable guidance and support throughout this journey.
